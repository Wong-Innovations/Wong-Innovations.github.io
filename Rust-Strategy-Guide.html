The Plan

Our group will have two teams in charge of resource gathering over the first few days of the server. J will head Team Scrap, while Wong heads Team Farm. Wonky and Jay can contribute to either team they want.

Day 1

  1. Join as early in wipe as possible.
  2. Assess the map and determine a prime base spot
    • Must be large and flat
    • Preferably close to outpost or high loot monuments
  3. Farm our respective resources as we run to the base location.
  4. Wong will put down our starter floorprint.

Days 1-3

  1. Grind scrap to get workbenches up and save comps for guns and deployables. (see recycling guide below)
  2. Farm up enough to get to early shooting floor up.
  3. Start gathering mats for compound and roof bunkers.
    • Jacky farm
    • Run furnaces constantly

Days 4-6

  1. Team Scrap will begin aquiring another 4 module car and bring it back to base.
  2. Take bradley.
  3. Finish Compounding the base and roof bunker.
  4. build car garage and wire up the base.

Day 7

  1. Finish shooting floor.
  2. Take heli.
  3. Begin our domination of the server.
